Secondary school?

New school, new teachers, new friends, new challenges, new subjects, new environment. So much pressure for a 13 year old kid. well, you're NOT ALONEEE. We've all been through that "first day of school feeling". The stress, excitement, anxiety, which all leads up to the feeling of throwing up or fainting at some point.
The first 2 months of school were amazing with the secondary 1 orientation camp, CCA fair, CCA trials and much more .

Until... COVID pandemic was announced a global pandemic >:(. And that was when the zoom classes, online work, offline work we had to do, came. But we were fortunate to have dedicated teachers who were there to ensure the lessons, homework, and assignments were done so that we would not be behind class when school started. When school resumed, they recorded lessons for those who couldn't go back to school and made sure that no student was left behind.
We really appreciate what our teachers have done for us !
