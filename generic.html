School logo

See the lion and the torch? Yes, thats our school's crest. The majestic lion symbolises courage, strenght and character. The visible strands on its mane represents the 5Ds The torch is an illustration of our schools motto "Knowledge, the torch of life". It also symbolises the commitment of the school to be a torch-bearer and trailblazer,an icon of hope and light for others

School Mission and vision

Vision

An Institution of Excellence which Nurtures Ethical Change-Makers.

Mission

To instil in our students the school values that shall be the hallmark of a Dunmanite.

The Dunmanite Creed

Together you and I will leave
As distinctive Dunmanites,
Discerning in our choice of right from what is not,
Disciplined in attitude and conduct;
Daring to experiment and forge breakthroughs and;
Determined in all that we do;
As it is our Duty
To serve and better our school, community and nation.

Philosophy

Every child has different abilities, can become an enquiring and independent learner, and can learn to accept responsibility for his learning outcome.
